How the updates work
Time-based simulation with animated map activity
This dashboard updates every second from a starting mileage total and a fixed daily growth rate. Crash avoidance, savings, and life impact are recalculated continuously. The activity maps pulse and the dots move over time to simulate ongoing operations without relying on external map libraries.
